# Analog Electronics Guide

## What this topic covers
- analog signals and waveform concepts
- amplifiers and op-amps
- filters and signal conditioning

## How to study it
1. Start with voltage, current, and signal behavior.
2. Study amplifier basics and feedback.
3. Learn how analog circuits prepare signals for sensors and controllers.

## Suggested practice
- measure analog sensor voltage
- test a simple amplifier circuit
- compare digital and analog signals

## Goal
Be able to work with real-world sensor and control signals.
