# Circuit Analysis Guide

## What this topic covers
- Ohm's law and Kirchhoff's laws
- series and parallel circuits
- voltage division and current division

## How to study it
1. Learn the core laws first.
2. Practice solving simple circuit problems manually.
3. Compare calculations with measurements.

## Suggested practice
- calculate expected voltage across a resistor
- verify your result with a multimeter
- analyze a simple LED circuit

## Goal
Develop the ability to understand and predict circuit behavior.
