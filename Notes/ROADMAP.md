# Notes Roadmap

Use these notes as your study companion while you work through the PDFs and topic folders.

## How to study the notes
1. Read each topic once for overview.
2. Highlight formulas, definitions, and key concepts.
3. Rewrite the ideas in your own words.
4. Connect each topic to a small robotics example.

## Suggested workflow
- Review notes before opening a PDF.
- Summarize each section after studying it.
- Keep a personal notebook of practical observations.

## Goal
Build a clear, personal understanding that can be applied to robot design.
