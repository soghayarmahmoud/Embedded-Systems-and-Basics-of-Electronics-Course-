# Project Roadmap

This roadmap is designed to guide your learning from foundational electronics to embedded systems and robotics.

## Phase 1: Foundations
- Understand voltage, current, resistance, and power.
- Study circuit laws and basic circuit behavior.
- Learn the role of resistors, capacitors, inductors, diodes, and transistors.

## Phase 2: Digital and Analog Thinking
- Learn analog electronics and signal behavior.
- Study digital logic, boolean algebra, and logic gates.
- Connect these concepts to sensors, controllers, and motor interfaces.

## Phase 3: Embedded Systems
- Learn embedded system architecture and basics.
- Study programming fundamentals for microcontrollers.
- Practice input/output, timers, interrupts, and ADC.

## Phase 4: Communication and Integration
- Understand UART, SPI, I2C, and CAN.
- Use communication protocols to connect sensors and actuators.
- Prepare for real-world embedded applications.

## Phase 5: Robotics Application
- Build simple hardware projects.
- Combine sensing, control, and actuation.
- Progress toward a fully functional robot.

## Suggested order
1. Basics of Electronics
2. Embedded Systems
3. Notes and PDF resources
4. Hands-on practice and robotics projects
